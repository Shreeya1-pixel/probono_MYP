Our Mission

To reach out to the youth of our country and enrol all citizens above 18 so they can actively participate in elections. We make voting more interesting for young India through micro-targeted campaigns that increase awareness and turnout.

visibility

Our Vision

A democracy with educated, politically aware voters — where every institution becomes a voter-friendly campus and the power of youth shapes the course of our nation.
