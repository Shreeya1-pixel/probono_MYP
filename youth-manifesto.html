Youth Manifesto

Igniting energy. Breaking barriers. Building collaboration.

The youth manifesto movement aims to bring together long-time youth advocates, environmentalists, activists, emerging leaders, artists, policymakers, and academics — all in one city — to inspire continuous change.

What We Stand For

To inspire continuous change, we must invest in youth leadership and harness young people's collective power and energy. The Youth Manifesto is a space for youth-led accountability — where the next generation defines the legislative agenda through grassroots consensus.

We are excited to announce the Youth Manifesto as a platform where young voices shape policy, hold leaders accountable, and build a democracy that reflects their aspirations.
